Aspects covered in the Sankalp

Infra. Development

Modernizing highways, airports, railways, and urban spaces to improve connectivity and ensure seamless access to world-class facilities. Improve access to clean drinking water, sanitation, and affordable housing.

Sustainable Energy

Advancing renewable energy, reducing emissions, and promoting clean energy solutions for a greener future. Implement policies for achieving Net Zero Emissions by 2070. Transition to renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydropower.

Digital India

Expanding internet access and technological innovations like AI and blockchain to build a digitally empowered society. Bridge the digital divide by ensuring rural and urban connectivity.

Agriculture Innovation

Boosting modern farming practices with technology, and strengthening food security. Double farmer incomes through better MSPs (Minimum Support Prices) and exports. Modernize agriculture with technology like drones and precision farming.

Education and Skills

Promote vocational training and skill-building under initiatives like Skill India. Reforming education systems and fostering skill development to equip the youth for a competitive global workforce. Reform school and higher education systems to align with global standards.

Healthcare

Strengthening hospitals, health insurance schemes, and preventive care to provide quality healthcare for every citizen. Strengthen the Ayushman Bharat health insurance program.

Economic Growth

Promoting industries, startups, and exports to establish India as a global economic leader while creating jobs and enhancing entrepreneurship opportunities. Boost manufacturing and exports under programs like Make In India. Strengthen small and medium enterprises (SMEs) to create jobs.

Defense and Security

A secure nation with advanced defense technology and strong cybersecurity with Soldiers, missiles, cybersecurity shields/icons. Strengthen domestic defense manufacturing under Aatmanirbhar Bharat. Enhance infrastructure and disaster resilience.
